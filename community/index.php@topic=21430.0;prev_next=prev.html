Darn it ! I haven't made it to BK Ice Cream Factory yet. Is that by the Brooklyn Bridge?

I think there are a couple of them, but the one I've been to is at the north end of Commercial St. in Greenpoint, at the very very northern tip of Manhattan Ave., near where Commercial, Manhattan and Ash St. meet.  Right over the Pulaski Bridge from LIC.  If you walk across the bridge, go down the pedestrian stairway before the end of the bridge and walk west on Ash St. one block.  It's in the space of the late, lamented Bleu Drawes, if anyone remembers them - an out-of-the way corner to find a Jamaican restaurant to be sure, but they still hold the prize for the best curried goat I've ever had.  (And no, the Brooklyn Ice Cream Factory does not offer curried goat ice cream.    )
