Title: Green Buildings Open House Tours on Saturday, October 6.
Post by: Criosote on October 05, 2007, 12:01:14 PM
Hi there!

Just wanted to let you know about this event.  GreenHomeNYC is a non-profit organization that advocates and teaches about green building technologies, and once a year, first Saturday in October, they organize tours throughout NYC to visit buildings and homes that use green technology.  They have walking tours, bus tours and bike tours.  There is a cost ranging from $20-$35.

Some of the tours are already sold out, but the one I am leading still has 5 spots.  Here is a link to more information:
http://www.greenhomenyc.org/page/tour2007
