I've done a little more research into the Credit Union idea. It seems that you need approximately 500 people to start up a credit union, which I think is very doable in Astoria. But another thing I was thinking is that it would be really useful even just to have a credit union ATM here in Astoria. You can then be a member of any Credit Union and make deposits at that ATM (they have an agreement among all Credit Unions).

Just got off the phone with a very rude person at Progressive Credit Union here in New York. I called just to ask about how one goes about requesting an ATM machine get put in somewhere in their neighborhood, and the person at the other end said "we're not interested" and slammed the phone down. Definitely won't be banking with them.

I'm going to see if there are any other credit unions that might like to have an ATM in Astoria. Maybe if we are able to help them find a space for the machine that's safe and cheap/free rent some bank might go for it.
