If you can get RCN, give them a try.  I have them and really like it.  I had Time Warner and the internet was terrible.  RCN came and completely rewired.  I guess if TW had rewired, that might have been OK, too, but the didn't.

I do have cable TV as well, so I can't give you a good idea of price, but they do have tiered service.  I do the 20Mbps, which is the top level residential and it's very fast...better than I have at work (a major corporation in Manhattan).  I believe they have even faster commercial plans, and slower more economical residential plans.

Also, I have been very successful negotiating lower fees/higher service with RCN.  A TW door-to-door salesman recently told me he couldn't come close to the deal I was getting.  It might be harder if you're only looking for internet, but it's worth a shot.

Good luck!
