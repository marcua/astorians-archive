Yup, I do the Triple Play package that Time Warner offers. Overall, I don't have many complaints. In fact, when it comes to Phone and 'Net I have virtually no complaints. From rare time to time, my phone does something odd (a wrong number when someone is convinced they dialed correct, my call going to the wrong number when I dial - but fixed quickly) and the internet never has any problems.

The cable is the biggest problem. I have to reset my cable box (unplug, replug. replug? whatever the term) about twice a month. Whenever TWC does have to come to my apartment, it is the way 3 hours and miss them if they ring and don't wait 30 second for you to get the door. Pain in my arse.

But overall, its worth the value and I don't have an substantial complaints. And, honestly, how often do we have power outages? Good luck!

The deal is really great-BUT-there is a catch that you should be aware of. My sis found out the hard way. To get the good pricing you have to lock into a contract for 2 years. Should you move during this 2 year period to a non-TWC service area you will have to pay an early cancellation fee for terminating the contract. My sis is still fighting with TWC on this one almost 7 months after she moved because it was never made clear to her when she signed up for the triple play. Just wanted to let everyone know.
