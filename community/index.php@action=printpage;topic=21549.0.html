About us:

P2P specializes in clicker training - a modern, scientifically-proven rewards-based method of dog training that does not use force or intimidation. The training is hands-free, loads of fun, and makes it easy for every member of your family to take on the role of "dog trainer". We work with all breeds, ages, temperaments, and sizes of dogs, as well as all types of behavior problems from housebreaking, barking, and leash walking, to more serious issues like aggression and fear-based behaviors. Group classes and socialization groups coming soon!

Meet the Trainer:

Ettel Edshteyn is a graduate of the Karen Pryor Academy and is a Certified Training Partner. She uses behavior theory and clicker training to help dogs and their people live together harmoniously and with mutual respect and trust. She continues to stay on the edge of new and innovative training theory by attending seminars and workshops held by the foremost trainers and scientists in the dog training and behavior field. She has experience working with a variety of dog breeds and their owners.
