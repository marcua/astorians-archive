Title: Free screening of Saint of 9/11
Post by: causidicus on January 18, 2007, 03:22:33 PM
Free movie screening for a great movie, co-produced by Astoria resident Brendan Fay--

Dear Friend,

District Leader Dorothy Phelan and I are pleased to announce that there will be a very special screening of the award winning film Saint of 9/11 on Thursday, January 25, 2007 at 7 PM at the Jewish Center of Jackson Heights, 37-06 77 Street, Jackson Heights, Queens.  Light refreshments will be served. (You can download the attached flier for details or to send it to friends.)

Following the screening, Co-Producer Brendan Fay will discuss the making of the film and the life of Father Mychal Judge who the film is about.  Father Judge was the first person to die in the attack on the World Trade Center on 9/11.  He was the FDNY chaplain at the time.

The film, which is narrated by Sir Ian McKellan, was an official selection at the 2006 Tribeca Film Festival, the 2006 Montreal World Film Festival and was a smashing success.  It has won many other awards.

My advice is to come early to get the best seats.  Admission is free.

HOLD THE DATE -  Thursday, March 1, 2007 - for a special screening of three short films produced by Mr. Fay who is also the founder of the Queens Saint Patrick's Parade, the topic of one of his short films.

If you have any questions, please feel free to call me at 718-457-2928.

I look forward to seeing you soon.

Sincerely,

Daniel Dromm
District Leader
39 Assembly District, Part A
