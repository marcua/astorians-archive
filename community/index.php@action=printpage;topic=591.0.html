MEDITATION ON THE MIND
3 Talks Introducing Buddhist Meditation and Psychology
 
Presented by Chakrasambara Buddhist Center
www.meditationinnewyork.org
212.902.6706

Sundays 12:00-2:00pm
With Luis Villalon
August 14        The Mind and its Potential
August 21        Clarity of Mind 
September 4    Mind and the Nature of Reality 

Our mind has an unlimited potential.  By meditating on the clear nature of the mind we learn to let go of tension and stress, and to experience freedom, confidence, and happiness.  Everyone is welcome.  ($10 per class)

Each class is self contained and can be taken individually.  Anyone can benefit from these
techniques regardless of spiritual background. 

At Grace Lutheran Church
31-20 21st Ave (at 32nd Street)
Astoria, NY
(Please use office entrance to the left of the main Church entrance)

By Train:
Take the N train to Ditmars, walk north on 31st Street to 21st Ave and make a right.
