I doubt there's some formal way of doing things at Rosario's, it's just not that type of place.  All I did was go to the back, ask the first guy I saw if he had any pizza, he said how many slices, and that was that.  They usually ring you up on a separate register so you don't wait in a long line.  When I have bought other things there, the guy speaks Italian, and immediately assumes you want something else from behind the counter, by asking "what else" after you hand him your stuff.  I think they just assume you know what you want, but perhaps they'll be open to questions or will make suggestions to you.  They do have all sorts of sauce, just not sure if it's homemade.  I got tomato/pesto sauce there once, it was really good.
