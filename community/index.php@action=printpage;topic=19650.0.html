Title: NYC Drinks Free Intro and Official Launch Party
Post by: nycdrinksfree on October 20, 2010, 12:01:51 PM
Hello there fellow Astorians,

Thanks for clicking through to find out more about me.

Im new to this forum and I can see that its fairly active and I hope to contribute
to this growing forum daily and just wanted to let people exactly know who I am.

My name is Felix and I run an events company called NYC Drinks Free.

NYC Drinks Free is an events company based out of Queens, NY.  It was started
by my partner Mike and myself.  Our focus is to provide people with a memorable
experience at some of the best bars and night clubs that New York has to offer,
primarily in Queens. 

We'd like to bring awareness to the fact that there's more to NY than just partying
in the city.  So we'll be hitting up all the boroughs.  We believe the nightlife scene in
Astoria is something worth talking about.

Currently, we are in the process of designing and hosting a blog that people can visit
to find out about hotspots all over NYC and information on legitimate open bars and
drink specials around town.

Also, NYC Drinks Free will be throwing exclusive parties and events that will only be
offered to members who subscribe to our private email list and facebook fan page.

For our next event, we are going to be throwing a Halloween Foam Launch Party
hosted at Amnesia Nightclub in Astoria, Queens.

This event will be the official launch party for NYC Drinks Free.

The event is on Saturday, October 30th, and there will be a lot going on.  There will be
a 2-hour open bar (from 10 PM-Midnight), live dj, live dancers, best costume contests,
alcoholic slushees, beer pong, foam, and even a contortionist. And at just a $20 cover,
it's sure to be a night to remember.

So we'd like to invite our fellow Astorians to come and join us for this massive event.  Just
visit our event page on Facebook to RSVP or for more details by going to the link below.

www.nycdrinksfree.com/halloween

I look forward to being a part of this wonderful community and we really hope to see all of
you at our event.

Thanks for reading.

Felix
