I have been here all my life ....38 years. I grew up on 34th street and Broadway. Now I live in  the area of the 40's street. Astoria to me is great. Great things to do here, great places to eat......and real estate is great as far as an investment. If I sold today, I would more than triple my money since  '91. Some say move to the island, upstate, or even Whitestone. I say, KEEP IT!!!!! I have the best of both worlds right here. We looked into Oyster Bay and Bayville. Sorry guys, but I've got a beef about this living in Astoria, then leaving it issue.  As someone who's been living here all my life (and as for my age, none of your business), I've watched the rents go up ridiculously for the past 10 years.  That's because all of a sudden the neighborhood became desirable for those from other areas who wanted to live close to the City but couldn't afford the even higher rents there.  So they may eventually leave Astoria, but those of us who don't want to do so are still stuck with rents that skyrocketed (and may be priced out of the neighborhood we love). 

Not blaming anybody, just had to vent some spleen.
