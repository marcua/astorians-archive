How frequently do you call 311?
« on: July 22, 2008, 02:57:13 PM »
I was pretty impressed when I read this article in the Queens Gazette about the SCOUT program: http://www.qgazette.com/news/2008/0716/features/002.html

It seems they report everything they see to 311.  The article got me thinking.  I never thought to call 311 to report something like graffiti.  In fact, I realized that I don't call 311 enough.  I avoid the same pot holes every day and walk past some graffiti wondering to myself "how did they get up there?" but have to admit that I have never called to report these types of things.

I'm hoping it would make a difference to call 311 and plan on making a more concerted effort to do so.  I'm wondering how many of you out there have reported quality of life issues to 311 and have seen any results.
