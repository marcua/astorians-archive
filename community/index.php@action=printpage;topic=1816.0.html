Title: Blessing of the Bikes- Sat. Apr. 22nd 9:30am
Post by: Christine on March 26, 2006, 01:16:50 AM
Save the date! This is when you can bring your bikes into St. John the Divine on Amsterdam and 112th St.

I'm willing to lead a ride from here in Astoria (very easy drive from L.I.) over the Triboro and into NYC- it's only a few miles each way and a terrific intro to city riding. Other sights include the "Seinfeld" diner.

Meet in Astoria Park lot by 8am, so we can roll by 8:30. Earlier if you want brunch at the Neptune Diner, though it's not a strenuous ride that requires carbo-loading. I could get a bunch o' bagels or we could stop at Dunkin' Donuts or something.

Directions:

Take the Northern State/GCP to Exit 3, Hoyt Ave.
Go straight ahead on Hoyt Ave. (parallel to Triboro Bridge, past the diner and under that train trestle) for about 1/2 mile.
Keep going straight, crossing over 21st St. This takes you directly into Astoria Park's main lot.

If you need further enticement, this is the world's largest cathedral of its kind: http://www.stjohndivine.org/

7th Annual Blessing of the Bicylcles
Sat., Apr. 22 at 9:30 a.m.
St. John the Divine
1047 Amsterdam Ave
New York, NY 10025

All cyclists and their friends, including bike messengers, bike racers, bike commuters, recreational bikers, and, of course, children, are invited to this colorful service, which includes a blessing to kick off a safe cycling season.

http://www.sfgate.com/cgi-bin/article.cgi?file=/chronicle/archive/2005/09/30/EBG6UERKK21.DTL&type=printable

***Keep in mind that the cathedral considers this a fundraiser, so bring a few spare dollars as a donation.***
