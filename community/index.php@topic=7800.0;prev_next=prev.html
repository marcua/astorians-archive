Hi all - Sorry that my very first post is to ask for something rather than contribute something to the community,  :oops: but I've got a quick PSA. I'm volunteering at the Astoria Park rest stop for the NY Century Bike Tour this Sunday, 9/8/07, and we're really running short of people to help out. To the best of my knowledge, all it entails is setting up a few tables, lugging a few boxes of food, cutting up bagels or fruit or whatever, and handing it out to hungry cyclists who have just ridden 55+ miles through Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. People are pretty friendly. Plus you get a t-shirt!

So if you're available for a few hours on Sunday and would like to lend a hand, please contact:

Oksana Mironova
Volunteer and Membership Outreach Coordinator
Transportation Alternatives
127 West 26th St, Suite 1002
New York, NY 10001
e. volunteer@transalt.org
t. (646) 873-6036
transalt.org

Thanks!
