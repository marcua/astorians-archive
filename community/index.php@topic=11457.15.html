Well...I'd say I got good results out of 311 but they really couldn't force my landlord to obey the law. Ideally, I would have had to call them once each winter and filed a complaint that our building's boiler was broken (again) and some enforcement department would have made sure it got fixed and stayed fixed through May. Instead, I had to call every three days or so when it would drop below the legal temperature again.

However, everyone I ever spoke with at 311 was at least marginally helpful though they're not the friendliest call center people I've ever spoken with. I think they're mostly sitting there with a big Googlewiki of NYC information, most of which I could look up myself, but I think it's a good service and I won't hesitate to use it again if I need it.
